Spring 2018: Tuesdays 12-2 pm room M/1.02

This is the official website of the informal analysis & PDE seminar at Cardiff University. This is different from the unofficial website of the formal seminar, which can be found here. The informal seminar is meant to be slow-paced, to allow members of the group to learn new topics, and to allow guests to expand on their "formal" seminar talks in an informal setting.
